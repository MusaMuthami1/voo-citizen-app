## Key Features
- ✅ Username creation during registration
- ✅ Strong password validation  
- ✅ OTP phone verification
- ✅ Time-based greeting (Morning/Afternoon/Evening)
- ✅ Profile shows name & phone from DB
- ✅ Images upload to Cloudinary
